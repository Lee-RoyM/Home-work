Cockatiel

Back

They are prized as household pets and companion parrots throughout the world and are relatively easy to breed. As a caged bird, cockatiels are second in popularity only to the budgerigar.

Mutations (list)

  1. Cinnamon
  2. Dilute, incorrectly known as Pastel Silver in the past
  3. Dominant Silver/Ashen Dilute
